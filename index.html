Blinkit · ZomatoMar 2025 – Present

Dark Store Digitization & Unit Economics Redesign Across 2,000+ Stores

The Problem

Blinkit's dark store network had grown fast — but layout logic, storage allocation, and replenishment flows hadn't kept pace. Stores had inconsistent layouts, no digitized location intelligence, and manual processes driving high expiry waste and unpredictable picking times.

My Approach

Field discovery across 50+ dark stores to understand root causes. Authored a full PRD and designed the end-to-end digitization flow. Built a location intelligence framework and prioritization logic engine deployable at chain scale, with an execution dashboard to track rollout and variance in real time.

Storage & Inventory Logic

Introduced FEFO-aligned batch-level storage logic to address expiry waste. Built 4 diagnostic heatmap tools for item placement. Automated storage-to-replenishment integration, eliminating manual data flows entirely.

Rollout

Led a 10-member cross-functional team — piloting, training, and standardizing the new operating model across 90% of the network.
